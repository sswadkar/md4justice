Maryland For Justice

Our Mission

At MD Justice, we want to make it easy for Marylanders to email their local officials about bills they want to be passed because we understand that you might not always have the right words to say, or the right people to contact. Just go to the “Email your Representative” tab or click the button below and quickly get a pre-written email ready to send.

Our Vision

As much as they say they do, representatives don’t listen to every single voice. This is partly because they don’t have the time to bring up every constituent’s concern, and that’s why support in large numbers is so important. That’s where we come in. Our vision is to provide a convenient method to gather support from people all over Maryland so that we can combine our efforts to bring reform to our state.
