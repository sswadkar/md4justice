Inclusive Schools Act

Discrimination is still prevalent in our school systems. The Inclusive Schools Act would create explicit anti-discrimination protections for all students in our Maryland school system. This act establishes a pathway for students and their families to work with MSDE to resolve the discriminatory action. The bill also requires schools to develop written policies for schools, employees and students regarding civil rights protections.
